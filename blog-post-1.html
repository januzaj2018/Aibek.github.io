Time Management

How To Manage Your Time As A Student

Avoid Cramming

Cramming the night before an exam is less efficient than preparing to exam for 4 hours spread out over a few weeks or a few month

Learn how to study effectively

In school or in university no one teaches us to learn effectively, we just study for ourselves being in lack of knowledge about efficient studying.

Success isn’t overnight. It’s when every day you get a little better than the day before. It all adds up.
Dwayne Johnson
Be experimental with self care time

Don’t be so depressed about studying, and just try to relax at your free time, therefore I recommend you to do things that help you to relax. Don’t watch Netflix or tv serials instead of this try to go to event in your school or university it’s cool and interesting time spending.

Schedule everything

When you scheduling your life you can see the free time that you have to do something unintentional, that you didn’t planned.

Time is only thing that we have to do things, so don’t squander you time scrolling TikTok or Instagram.

Even if you have a little blocks of time for example a 15 minute that you have between classes is valuable and it’s literally a lot of time. You can spend this time reviewing flashcards or just walking around without music, for kind of relaxation.

Be satisfied of done work

You have to choose be satisfied of your works that you have completed during a day, so don’t be so nervous about unfinished works. And don’t blame yourself saying that you didn’t have done enough work. Just relax it’s a simply individual day, the other days not gonna be like this day.
